Privacy Policy – Kamna Techno Pvt. Ltd.

At Kamna Techno Pvt. Ltd., located in Sector 2, Noida, your privacy is extremely important to us. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website or use any of our services, including video editing, corporate film production, social media video creation, and motion graphics.

Information We Collect

Personal Information: Name, phone number, email address, company name (when you fill out contact or inquiry forms)

Project-related Data: Footage, images, scripts, and creative briefs shared for editing or production

Website Usage Data: Browser type, pages visited, and IP address through cookies

How We Use Your Information

Deliver personalized video editing services

Respond to your queries or service requests

Improve our website performance and user experience

Communicate important updates or promotions

Maintain records for billing and support purposes

Data Security

We use secure servers, encrypted storage, and strict access control to keep your data safe. Your footage and files are only accessed by authorized editors working on your project.

We Never

Sell or rent your data to third parties

Use your footage for promotional purposes without written consent

Spam your inbox

Third-party Tools & Services

We may use trusted third-party services like:

Google Analytics (to track website performance)

Payment gateways (for billing)

Cloud storage (for secure file exchange)

These platforms follow their own privacy policies and comply with global data protection laws.

Your Rights

Request access to your data
